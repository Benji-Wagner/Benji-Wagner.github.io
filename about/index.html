Family

Although I have a somewhat large family (I can't keep up with my mom's family tree), I only really talk to immediate family. This is in part because of how spread out my family is. At the moment, my mom lives in Arizona, my brother lives in Chicago, his fiancee lives in LA, my dad lives in Maryland, my mom's family lives in parts of Malaysia, Philadelphia, and California, and my dad's family lives in rural Minnesota. Phew!

I took family for granted up until the end of my undergraduate career. Now I enjoy taking trips up to Maryland to visit my dad, and I try to fly out to Arizona when I have breaks. I think I started to appreciate family when I was blessed with two baby sisters. Hanna and Esther inspire me to be the best version of myself, and I hope that they will look up to me as they grow older. Also, those girls know how to have fun and make a mess everyday. It's awesome!
